Services

I work on the messy, high-friction cases that drain your staff and stall your revenue. The goal is not to replace your team, but to clear the problems they never have time to finish.

Referral & Authorization Management

For clinics and practices with repeated complaints that “the referral never came through” or “we’re still waiting on auth.”

  • Centralized tracking log for outbound referrals and requests
  • Verification calls to receiving offices and plans
  • Support for resolving missing information or mismatched data
  • Weekly status summary for leadership

Denial & Escalation Packet Construction

For individual cases that are worth fighting for—financially or reputationally—but lack the documentation structure needed for serious review.

  • Timeline reconstruction across records, EOBs, and correspondence
  • Identification of key issues, contradictions, and missing pieces
  • Drafting of escalation letters or appeal summaries for your team to send

Problem Claim / Case Backlog Cleanup

For teams sitting on a backlog of unresolved, “someone should look at this” files.

  • Intake and categorization by type and risk
  • Quick wins vs. deep-dive identification
  • Execution on selected cases within a defined sprint

Government & Program Communication Support

For organizations that regularly interact with public programs, boards, or oversight agencies and need clean, consistent documentation.

  • Form completion support (using information you provide)
  • Packet assembly for complaints, appeals, or reviews
  • Cover letters and summaries prepared for your signature
