Pricing

Every organization is different. These ranges exist so you can quickly see whether this type of engagement makes sense before we speak.

Targeted Cleanup Sprint

Best for backlogged “problem” files.

  • Defined set of cases or issues
  • Time-bound engagement with clear deliverables
  • Status summary and recommended next steps

Ideal when you want to test this support on a small, contained scope.

Ongoing Administrative Breakdown Support

For clinics, firms, or billing teams with continuous need.

  • Monthly allocation of cases or hours
  • Rolling intake of new breakdowns and denials
  • Regular reporting and strategy check-ins

Designed to function as an external, specialized operations lane your team can rely on every month.

High-Impact Case Packets

For select, complex matters only.

  • Deep documentation review
  • Chronology and issue mapping
  • Escalation-ready summaries for your leadership or counsel

Engagement limited by capacity; reserved for matters with clear strategic value.

For a more precise estimate, request a scope call and outline your current breakdowns, volume, and internal capacity.
